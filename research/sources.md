# Sources — AI-Powered SEO Content Production for B2B SaaS

> Research project mapping the strongest voices on AI-powered SEO content
> production for B2B SaaS in 2026. Curated for signal, not follower counts.

**Last updated:** 2026-05-19

## Selection criteria

Every expert clears at least three of four bars:
1. **Ships work** — operates an agency, builds a tool, or runs in-house at scale
2. **Public content stream** — LinkedIn, YouTube, podcast, or newsletter
3. **At least one of:** original research, named clients, owned product, 5+ yrs operating
4. **Active in the last 90 days**

The 10 span six strategic lenses (Data, Strategy, Curation, Tool, Technical,
Production, Ranks-first, Research, Practical, Executive). A playbook resolved
against one lens is a tactic book, not a playbook.
